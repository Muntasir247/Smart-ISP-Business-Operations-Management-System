Notice Period Rules
By employment type and seniority
Full-time employees must serve a minimum 30-day notice period before leaving.
Managers and senior-level positions require 60-day notice.
Part-time / contractual employees require 14-day notice.
During probation period, only 7 days notice is required from either party.
Notice period may be waived by mutual agreement with management approval.
2
Final Settlement & Dues
Salary clearance and dues
Final salary must be paid within 7 working days after last working day.
Unused annual leave (up to 30 days) will be encashed at basic salary rate.
Provident fund contribution will be released as per company policy.
Any outstanding loans or advances will be deducted from final settlement.
3
Exit Clearance Process
Mandatory clearance departments
IT: Return all company assets ‚Äî laptop, phone, access cards, dongles.
Finance: Clear all outstanding expenses, petty cash, company card balance.
HR: Submit resignation letter, complete knowledge transfer documentation.
Manager: Handover all pending work, credentials, and project documentation.
Security: Deactivate system access, email, and building entry on last day.
4
Immediate Termination Conditions
Grounds for instant dismissal
Gross misconduct or violation of company code of conduct.
Unauthorized disclosure of confidential company information.
Fraud, theft, or dishonesty in any form.
Consecutive unexplained absence for more than 5 days.
